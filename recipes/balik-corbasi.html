Turkish Fish Soup

Description

Put fish, salt, parsley, onion, garlic, and water
into a large pot and allow the magic to happen...

In another pot, heat olive oil over low heat,
then stir and allow more magic happen...

Ingredients

  • 4 lbs. firm white fish
  • 2 tsp. salt
  • 1 cup, coarsely chopped parsley (for stock)

Steps

  1. Wait for me
  2. Do no laugh
  3. Lick your lips
